Meet Yogmantra

Hi there, I am Tiyasha, a yoga instructor, and the founder of Yogmantra. I started this journey one years ago because, I was once like most people today who need fitness or a lifestyle that will make them healthy, flexible and feel better.

Yoga/Stretching is a tool that you can use whichever way you want.

I used to be highly impatient, and anxiety ridden, which were bred from varying life challenges and health issues, I deal with daily. This made living feel too rigid and too restricted. There is a saying, "if you can't be flexible in life, you become irritable with life" So, I decided to sought peace in order to lead a better life and thus, become healthier. Thanks to Yoga and a healthy lifestyle, I have been able to achieve this.

"if you can't be flexible in life, you become irritable with life"

The first thing people say to me when they discover I teach yoga is, "I'm not flexible enough to start" I often tell them "that's the more reason why you should start".

Yoga/Stretching is a tool that you can use whichever way you want. I use yoga as a form of relaxation, to enhance my flexibility and to calm my nerves.

"...stretching is more than just loosening muscles;"

I have learnt that stretching is more than just loosening muscles; it also helps to enhance blood flow, through the Arteries, veins and nerves. Stretching is about knowing yourself, and the causes of your pain and finding healing through limbering movement and a lifestyle change.

You can choose how much personality you want to infuse into your practice, when you come to the mat.
