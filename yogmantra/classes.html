Yoga for Athritis

Exercise works all kind of wonder in the human body, from stabilizing joints to improving muscle mass to reducing inflammation. It sounds good when you have no aches holding you back. Not doing anything when arthritis hit is worst than trying little stretches here and there. Yoga may cause painful flare ups in the short terms because more mobility is required from the joints; a little discomfort is acceptable while doing yoga stretch that means the joints are loosening up.
