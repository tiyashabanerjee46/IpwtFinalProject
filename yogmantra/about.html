About Yogmantra

Our Story

At yogmantra, we help you to release positive energy into the art of living and immerse yourself in holistic peace.

We found that Yoga is the greatest recipe to find balance between your mind and body, transforming their communication into one voice.

What kind of Yoga do we practice?

Achieve the impossible

We do not confine ourselves to a particular style but rather, we incorporate a variety of yoga into your regular practice, to enable you choose the styles that resonate with you more. We believe this will give our students a holistic experience in the pursuit of peace.

everyday practice

Our mission is to bring flexibility, relaxation and a healthy lifestyle change: through yoga stretches, breathing exercises, deep relaxation Peace

live what you love

Our teachings are based solely on making the mind communicate with the body freely and with our passionate and experienced Yoga trainers, you will be guided on Yoga styles best tailored for your level/needs and giving advice on other styles.
